Never attribute findings, decisions, or recommendations to a council, specialist, agent, or multi-agent debate in external-facing output. PRs, commit messages, issue descriptions, Slack messages, and any communication to humans should present the information directly.

Write: "The validator ignores minLength checks."
Not: "The council found that the validator ignores minLength checks."
Not: "The DevOps specialist recommended adopting prek."

Councils are an internal reasoning tool. The output is what matters, not how it was produced.
